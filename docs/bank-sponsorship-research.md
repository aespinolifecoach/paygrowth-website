# Bank Sponsorship Research for ISO Payments Processor

## Executive Summary

An ISO (Independent Sales Organization) cannot process payment transactions without an **acquiring bank sponsor**. The acquiring bank provides the essential banking relationship required to settle transactions, move funds, and maintain compliance with card network rules. This document provides comprehensive research on acquiring banks that offer ISO sponsorship programs, their requirements, fees, application processes, and the card network registration process.

**Key Findings:**
- Bank sponsorship is the single biggest hurdle for new ISOs
- Capital requirements range from $25,000 to $1,000,000+ depending on bank tier
- Application timelines range from 3-12 months
- Regional and community banks are most accessible to new ISOs
- Payment processors offer an alternative pathway with lower requirements

## 2. Bank Sponsorship Requirements

### 2.1 Minimum Capital Requirements

| Bank Tier | Minimum Capital | Notes |
|-----------|----------------|-------|
| **Large National** | $500,000 - $1,000,000+ | Very strict, prefer established companies |
| **Regional Bank** | $100,000 - $500,000 | Moderate requirements |
| **Community Bank** | $25,000 - $100,000 | More accessible |
| **Payment Processor** | $10,000 - $50,000 | Usually lower, adds margin |

### 2.2 Business Requirements

| Requirement | Large Bank | Regional Bank | Community Bank | Processor |
|------------|-----------|---------------|----------------|-----------|
| **Years in Business** | 5+ years | 3-5 years | 1-3 years | 0-1 year |
| **Annual Revenue** | $5M+ | $1M+ | Any | Any |
| **Audited Financials** | 3 years | 2 years | 1 year | Business plan |
| **Processing Volume** | $50M+/year | $10M+/year | $1M+/year | None minimum |
| **Legal Structure** | Corporation | LLC/Corp | LLC/Corp | Any |
| **State Registration** | All states | Select states | Operating states | Any |

### 2.3 Personal Requirements for Principals

| Requirement | Details |
|------------|---------|
| **Personal Credit Score** | 680+ typically required |
| **Criminal Background** | Clean record required |
| **Banking History** | Good standing with existing banks |
| **Net Worth** | $500K+ liquid assets for principals often required |
| **Payment Experience** | Industry experience strongly preferred |
| **Background Checks** | All principals with 10%+ ownership |

### 2.4 Technology/Infrastructure Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|------------|---------|
| **PCI DSS Compliance** | Evidence of compliance capability (SAQ D or QSA) |
| **Secure Environment** | PCI-compliant data handling infrastructure |
| **Network Security** | Firewall, encryption, vulnerability management |
| **Processing Platform** | Gateway, POS, or integrated solution |
| **Disaster Recovery** | Business continuity plan |
| **Monitoring** | 24/7 transaction monitoring |

### 2.5 Insurance Requirements

| Insurance Type | Typical Requirement |
|----------------|---------------------|
| **E&O Insurance** | $1M-$2M coverage minimum |
| **General Liability** | $1M minimum |
| **Cyber Liability** | $1M minimum (often required) |

## 3. Sponsorship Fees and Terms

### 3.1 One-Time Fees

| Fee Type | Amount | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| **Application Fee** | $0 - $10,000 | Sometimeswaived |
| **Due Diligence Fee** | $2,500 - $25,000 | Covers bank investigation |
| **Set-up Fee** | $5,000 - $50,000 | Technical and compliance setup |
| **Technology Integration** | $5,000 - $25,000 | Gateway certification, API setup |
| **Initial Reserve Deposit** | $10,000 - $100,000 | Refundable after term |

### 3.2 Ongoing Fees (Monthly/Annual)

| Fee Type | Amount | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| **Sponsorship Fee** | $500 - $5,000/month | Access to bank relationship |
| **Compliance Audit Fee** | $500 - $2,000/month | Ongoing compliance monitoring |
| **Network Access Fee** | $100 - $500/month | Card network access |
| **Annual Fee** | $1,000 - $5,000/year | Renewal and registration |
| **Minimum Volume Fee** | $250 - $1,000/month | If below minimum volume |

### 3.3 Reserve Requirements

| Reserve Type | Amount | Duration |
|--------------|--------|----------|
| **Rolling Reserve** | 5-10% of volume | Held 90-180 days |
| **Cash Reserve** | $10,000 - $100,000 | Held in escrow |
| **Chargeback Reserve** | $5,000 - $50,000 | Until termination |

### 3.4 Contract Terms

| Term | Common | Early Termination Fee |
|------|--------|---------------------|
| **1 year** | Entry-level | $5,000 - $25,000 |
| **3 years** | Standard | $10,000 - $50,000 |
| **5 years** | Often with better rates | $25,000 - $100,000+ |

### 3.5 Revenue Sharing Models

| Model | Description | Typical Split |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| **Interchange Pass-Through** | ISO pays exact interchange + markup | Bank takes 10-30 bps |
| **Residual Sharing** | Split of margin after interchange | ISO keeps 60-80% |
| **Flat Transaction Fee** | Fixed per-transaction fee to bank | $0.01-$0.05/transactions |
| **Hybrid** | Combination of pass-through + base fee | Varies |

## 4. Application Process and Timeline

### 4.1 Step-by-Step Process

```
Step 1: Initial Inquiry (Week 1-2)
├── Contact bank's business development team
├── Submit preliminary questionnaire
└── Initial screening conversation

Step 2: Information Package (Week 2-4)
├── Business formation documents
├── Financial statements (2-3 years audited)
├── Detailed business plan with projections
├── Personal financial statements of all principals
├── Background check authorizations
├── PCI DSS compliance documentation
├── Insurance certificates
└── References (bank, business, industry)

Step 3: Due Diligence Review (Week 4-12)
├── Credit check on business and all principals
├── Banking reference checks
├── Industry/business references
├── Background investigation
├── Compliance and risk review
├── Legal review of business structure
└── Technical platform assessment

Step 4: Contract Negotiation (Week 12-16)
├── Sponsorship agreement terms negotiation
├── Fee structure discussion
├── Reserve requirements
├── Liability limits and indemnification
├── Service level agreements
├── Exclusivity terms (review carefully)
└── Contract review by your attorney

Step 5: Technical Integration (Week 16-24)
├── Gateway certification process
├── API integration development
├── Compliance testing
├── Network certification testing
├── Security assessment
└── Full transaction testing

Step 6: Go Live (Week 24+)
├── Bank final approval
├── Card network registration
├── Staff training
├── Merchant onboarding setup
└── Official launch
```

### 4.2 Timeline by Bank Type

| Bank Type | Application to Approval | Technical Integration | Total Time |
|-----------|------------------------|--------------------| ------------|
| **Large National** | 6-12 months | 3-6 months | 9-18 months |
| **Regional Bank** | 3-6 months | 2-3 months | 5-9 months |
| **Community Bank** | 1-3 months | 1-2 months | 2-5 months |
| **Payment Processor** | 1-4 weeks | 2-8 weeks | 1-3 months |

## 7. Visa and Mastercard ISO Registration Process

### 7.1 Visa ISO/MSP Registration

**Registration Portal:** Visa Online SPI (Service Provider Interface)
- URL: https://www.visaonline.com/spiweb/

**Process:**
1. **Obtain Bank Sponsor** - Must have Visa member bank sponsor first
2. **Create Visa Online Account** - Access SPI portal
3. **Submit Application** - Online application with business details
4. **Pay Registration Fee** - $100-$500 annually (varies by program)
5. **Complete Compliance** - PCI DSS and security questionnaire
6. **Receive ISO/MSP Number** - Official registration with Visa

**Requirements:**
- Valid bank sponsor confirmation letter
- Business registration documents
- Financial statements
- Background checks on principals
- PCI DSS compliance documentation
- Security questionnaire completed

**Timeline:** 2-6 weeks after bank sponsorship confirmed

### 7.2 Mastercard ISO/MSP Registration

**Registration Portal:** Mastercard MSP Enrollment
- URL: https://www.mastercard.us/ (through member banks)

**Process:**
1. **Bank Sponsorship Required** - Mastercard member bank sponsor
2. **Submit Enrollment Application** - Through bank or directly
3. **Pay Registration Fee** - Varies by sponsor, typically $100-$500
4. **Complete Questionnaires** - Security, compliance, business
5. **Receive MSP Number** - Official registration with Mastercard

**Requirements:**
- Sponsorship confirmation from Mastercard member
- Business formation documents
- Financial capability evidence
- Background investigations
- PCI DSS compliance

**Timeline:** 2-6 weeks after bank sponsorship confirmed

### 7.3 Registration Costs Summary

| Item | Visa | Mastercard | Notes |
|------|------|------------|-------|
| **Application Fee** | $0-$500 | $0-$500 | Varies by bank |

### 7.4 Timeline from Application to Going Live

```

```

**Total Timeline: 3-9 months** depending on bank and complexity

## 8. Key Findings and Recommendations

### For PayGrowth (Startup ISO)

**Best Pathway:** Start with a payment processor that has existing bank relationships, then transition to direct bank sponsorship as volume grows.

**Recommended Steps:**
1. **Immediate (Month 1):** Apply to NMI (via Celtic Bank) or USAePay (via BofA)
2. **Short-term (Month 2-6):** Build volume, establish track record
3. **Medium-term (Month 6-12):** Apply for direct bank sponsorship with Celtic Bank, WebBank, or Murray Bank
4. **Long-term (Year 2+):** Transition to Tier 2 regional bank if conditions favorable

**Capital Requirements:**
- Minimum: $25,000-$50,000 (for processor-based ISO)
- Recommended: $100,000-$250,000 (for direct bank sponsorship)

### Questions to Ask Potential Sponsors

1. What are your minimum capital requirements?
2. What processing volume do you expect?
3. How long is the application process?
4. What are the all-in sponsorship fees?
5. What reserve requirements exist?
6. How is residual income shared?
7. What PCI DSS support do you provide?
8. Can you provide references from existing ISOs?
9. What is the contract term and early termination fee?
10. Do you allow work with other processors or card brands?
